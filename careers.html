Litigation Associate

Associates work directly with our partner attorneys to anticipate and outperform the expectations of our clients. We believe that practicing litigation is the best path to becoming a strong attorney. You will focus on trial-readiness and finding cost-efficient results for clients while identifying critical issues as soon as possible and using them to their maximum advantage in the litigation and negotiation processes.

Requirements
  • Recent graduates preferred
  • Assertive and outspoken demeanor
  • Highly intelligent and creative thinker
  • Willing to travel as required
  • Leadership experience preferred

Business Development Associate

Business Development Associates are responsible for supporting the legal team with managing the end to end client sales process, which includes sourcing and effectively qualifying new client leads; pitching to clients for business; establishing new and long-term client relationships; and developing client briefs. The business development lifecycle is fast-moving and requires an individual with drive and tenacity.

Requirements
  • Strong presentation, multitasking and customer service skills
  • Positive, proactive attitude and ability to work well in teams
  • Proven record of maintaining strong relationships with senior members of client organizations

Legal Assistant

Legal assistants' responsibilities include assisting lawyers with preparing any necessary documents for witness interviews, depositions, hearings, trials, and arbitration. Legal assistants are also responsible for locating and reviewing information applicable to a particular cases, persons, or subjects through online research and/or document reviews.

Requirements
  • Experience as a litigation legal assistant or paralegal in a legal department or law firm
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office and Google Suite
  • Experience with research technology including CDAsia and internet search
  • Willing to travel as required
  • Strong English oral and written communication

Law Student

Law students will work on real cases and transactions involving challenging legal issues. Working directly with our legal team, you will get stimulating and substantive client experience as an early set up for your career. As a result, you will be more prepared for life after the bar than your peers.

Requirements
  • Current student at accredited law school
  • Strong English oral and written communication
  • Positive, proactive attitude and ability to work well in teams
