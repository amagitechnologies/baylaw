We Are Bay Law Group

Bay Law Group has extensive experience in civil law, corporate and legal compliance, technology law, and litigation. We work closely with our clients to resolve their challenges and disputes to ensure they're protected and their resources are conserved.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

Trial Litigation

Our attorneys are the experts you want advocating on your side in court. We are always ready to aggressively defend our corporate and individual clients or prosecute on their behalf. We align our incentives with yours to seek the best solutions to get you back to normalcy.

Technology Law

Startups, cryptocurrency exchanges, and more come to us for help navigating evolving regulations around the world. We ensure technology-focused firms are compliant with modern regulations related to the DPA, GDPR, CFAA, PDPA, KYC/AML/CFT, and more.

Data Privacy Consultancy

We've partnered with AMAGI, one of the top software companies in the Philippines, to offer complete Data Privacy assurance for your business to ensure that your data is protected and your business is fully compliant with modern standards. Learn more here.

Corporate Law

From setting up an optimal corporate structure to helping you navigate changing regulations or hostile disputes, we ensure your business is built to thrive. We specialize in businesses of all types including those new to the Philippines or involved in digital commerce.

Family & Personal Law

We are well-practiced in creating sustainable, new starts for our clients whether they're immigrating to a new country, making a legal separation to pursue another path, or planning for their descendants.

Community Involvement

Our attorneys have a history in practicing in opposition to corruption, fighting for free speech rights, and assisting industry association in advocating for progressive, modern public policies.
