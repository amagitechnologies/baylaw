Your Legal Team

We Are Bay Law Group

Bay Law Group has extensive experience in civil law, corporate and legal compliance, technology law, and litigation. We ensure that clients get the results they need at the fastest possible time.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your business

Trial Litigation

Our attorneys are experts in court. We are always ready to aggressively defend our corporate and individual clients or prosecute on their behalf. We align our incentives with yours and seek the best solutions to get you back to normalcy as soon as possible.

Technology

Cryptocurrencies, exchanges, startups, and more come to us to help them navigate the new regulations evolving around the world. We will work and advocate on your behalf to ensure you are sufficiently protected as you innovate to the benefit of the world.

Corporate

From setting the optimal corporate structure to protecting your interests in labor disputes to ensuring your protection with regard to your contracts, operations, and new regulations in areas like Data Privacy, we ensure your organization is built to thrive.

Family & Personal

We enjoy creating sustainable, new starts for our clients whether they're immigrating to a new country, making a legal separation to pursue another path, or plan for their descendants. Allow us to walk you through the process to determine the most sustainable solution for your future.

Efficiency

Our success relies on the success of our clients. We combine our creative, legal expertise with technology and the right incentives to ensure you are protected in the least amount of your time and effort. Your time and peace of mind are worth it.

Community Involvement

We are proud to stand up for the rights of the unjustly accused. Our attorneys have a history in practicing in opposition to corruption, fighting for free speech rights, and assisting in draft progressive, modern policy. We go above and beyond in every effort we make.
