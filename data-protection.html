Team of Experts

Your Competitive Advantage

Save yourself the hassle, embarrassment, and costs due to possible violations of the Data Privacy Act (Philippines), General Data Protection Regulation (European Union), Personal Data and Protection Regulations (Singapore), and other regulations.

We believe that well executed data protection creates real competitive advantages for your company. Efficient and well-documented data protection makes your company not only more secure but also more attractive to business partners, customers and employees.

We assist you in developing all relevant corporate documentation and processes in an efficient and uncomplicated manner to enable you to provide a high level of data protection to your partners, employees, and customers.

Our Data Protection Services

External Data Protection Officer
For most businesses it’s prudent to appoint an external Data Protection Officer so that management and employees can concentrate on their core competencies. Depending on the needs of your company, you can have us handle all your data protection issues.
Data Protection Audit
With a data protection audit we will review the current status of the implementation of data protection regulations within your company. Our auditors work directly with regulatory authorities and know what they pay attention to during inspections.
Corporate Policies
Our experts gladly assist you with the creation and implementation of new or improved data protection policies, security policies, authorisation policies, statement of obligation, emergency procedures, etc.
Data Protection Training
More than giving just a bland seminar, our team will work closely with the personnel in your company that are most responsible and liable for the protection of your collected data. We adapt our training to your specific context.
Data Protection Emergency Assistance
When a problem occurs and sensitive, personally identifiable data lands in the wrong hands, expertise and finesse in data protection law are required. Our experts help you with data breaches or suspected data protection abuse.
Special Consultations
Does your organization work with extremely sensitive or complex data collection methods? Have you encountered a breach of your data? Are you collecting data from multiple jurisdictions? Contact us for a quote to guide you through overcoming your data protection challenges at any scale or scope.
