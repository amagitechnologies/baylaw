PRIVACY POLICY

We have written this policy to explain how Bay Law Group (“Bay Law”) uses the data you give us through our site, Baylaw.io.

    By providing personal information you are deemed to consent to your personal information being shared outside Bay Law in the following circumstances:
  • where Bay Law needs to share your information to provide the service you have requested.
  • where Bay Law needs to send the information to third parties who work on behalf of Bay Law to provide a service to you. We will only provide the information they need to deliver the service, and they are prohibited from using that information for any other purpose.
  • where Bay Law wants to keep you up to date on the latest service announcements, updates or other Bay Law information.
  • where you have given us permission to share your information with third parties.
  • Bay Law will also disclose your personal information if required to do so by law in urgent circumstances, to protect personal safety, the public or our website.

We may make anonymized information available to a third party for research purposes. You have the right to request that the information you gave be modified, altered or destroyed, upon proper notice to Bay Law on the contact number provided in the site. Also consider that if you choose to enable Bay Law to publish on your behalf to a third party, anything published will be subject to that third party's privacy policy. We require third parties to respect your privacy, and your agreement with a third party will control how your information is used, stored, and transferred by the third party.

There are a few types of information on Bay Law.

Should you wish to send an inquiry, the site will ask you to provide your name and email address in order for Bay Law to contact you. You will also be asked to provide a subject to give us an idea about your concerns. Lastly, a space will provided for you to provide additional information or comments in connection to your inquiry.

You are entitled to all the rights provided under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, including but not limited to, the right to amend, modify, alter or destroy the data you have provided us.

This policy applies each time you submit data to the site. Your use of the site means that you agree that we may use that data in accordance with this policy. Our privacy policy will change from time to time. The up to date privacy policy will always be shown on this page.

Your Data
